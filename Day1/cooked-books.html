Engagement Briefing · Vane & Mercer LLP

You've been assigned to the case.

You're the newest forensic auditor at Vane & Mercer, and the regulator just dropped a stack of files on your desk. Several companies are posting numbers that look too clean — soaring profits, glowing notes, spotless balance sheets. Management swears everything is fine.

Your job: walk the audit floor, open every statement, and flag the figures that don't add up — without accusing the innocent. The clues hide across the files: a number on one page is often given away by a note on another. Careers end over audits like this. Make sure it isn't yours.

How to play

  1. 1Move with WASD / arrow keys — or the on-screen pad. (Click the office first.)
  2. 2Walk to a desk and press E (or click it) to open a file.
  3. 3Click suspicious numbers or notes to flag them 🚩. Click again to un-flag.
  4. 4Cross-check the files — compare profit with cash flow; let the notes explain odd numbers.
  5. 5Open all files, then go to the Audit Desk and submit your report.
