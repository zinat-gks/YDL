🏦 Central Bank Tycoon

Run the nation's central bank. Set the interest rate, survive economic shocks, watch your decisions ripple through a living 3D city, and keep inflation near 2% with jobs intact.

How to play: Each quarter you set the interest rate and face a random economic event with real trade-offs. Raise rates to cool inflation (jobs suffer); cut them to fight unemployment (prices heat up). Your choices move seven stats — inflation, unemployment, GDP, credibility, public trust, market confidence and financial stability — and the 3D city reacts in real time. Explore your office, unlock achievements, and earn one of several endings.
